Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer catering services for events?
Yes, we offer catering services for corporate events, weddings, and private parties. Our mobile coffee bar can be set up at your location. Please contact us at least two weeks in advance for arrangements.
Are your coffee beans ethically sourced?
Absolutely! We're committed to ethical sourcing practices. All our coffee beans are sourced from farms that practice fair trade and sustainable farming methods. We maintain direct relationships with our farmers to ensure quality and fair compensation.
Do you have gluten-free pastry options?
Yes, we offer a selection of gluten-free pastries and snacks. Please ask our staff for recommendations, and always inform us of any allergies so we can ensure your safety.
Can I reserve seating for a group?
Yes, we accept reservations for groups of 6 or more. Please call the location you plan to visit at least 24 hours in advance to make arrangements. For larger groups, we recommend our private meeting room which can be reserved for a small fee.
