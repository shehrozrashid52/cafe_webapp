How It All Began

Artisan Coffee was born from a simple love for exceptional coffee and a desire to create community spaces where people could connect over great brews. Our journey started in 2010 when founders Sarah and Michael returned from a trip to coffee farms in Colombia and Ethiopia, inspired to bring authentic coffee experiences to their hometown.

What began as a small cart at the local farmers' market has grown into a beloved local chain of coffee houses, but our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community has never wavered.

Today, we continue to source the finest beans directly from farmers who share our values, roast in small batches to highlight each bean's unique character, and train our baristas to craft the perfect cup every time.

Our Values

Sustainability

We're committed to ethical sourcing, reducing our environmental footprint, and supporting coffee farming communities through fair trade practices.

Quality

From bean to cup, we never compromise on quality. Each coffee is carefully selected, roasted to perfection, and brewed with precision.

Community

We believe coffee brings people together. Our spaces are designed to foster connection, creativity, and conversation.

Innovation

While honoring tradition, we continually explore new brewing methods, flavors, and experiences to delight our customers.

Care

We care deeply about our customers, our team, our farmers, and our planet. This care guides every decision we make.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from customer service to coffee preparation to store design.
